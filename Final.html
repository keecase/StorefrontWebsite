About KayCee Abstract

Painting is something that not only interests me but, at times, saves me. My name is Kiely, and I am the owner and creator of KayCee abstract. Sometimes the world can seem like it is just too much to process, and for a long time, I did not know how to deal with it. Finally, I found painting. When I thought about painting a picture, I thought it had to start with an image and finish that exact way. What I realized, though, is that painting can just flow. It can just be a release of feelings onto a canvas, and whatever the finished product is, it will be precisely what it needs to be in that moment. Painting then became less about trying to accomplish something and more about realizing something. Things can become so much more clear when you take a moment to look. The abstract is beautiful. The perfectly imperfect is the best way of life. I always remind myself that there is inspiration everywhere. All you have to do is take a deep breath and allow yourself to see it.

Thank you for reaching out !
